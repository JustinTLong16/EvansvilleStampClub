About the Club

Eight decades of philately on the Ohio.

Founded in the 1930s, the Evansville Stamp Club has been bringing collectors together for nearly a century. We're a nonprofit, all-volunteer organization dedicated to promoting the hobby of stamp collecting through monthly meetings, educational programs, and community outreach.

Our members range from casual hobbyists to award-winning exhibitors, and we host one of the most respected regional shows in the Midwest — EVANSPEX, Indiana's longest-running stamp and postcard show.

What we do

More than just trading stamps.

A welcoming space to learn, share, and grow your collection — no matter your level.

🤝

Monthly Meetings

Join us the third Tuesday of every month for programs, swaps, auctions, and great conversation with fellow collectors.

🎓

Beginner Mentoring

New to philately? We pair newer collectors with experienced members to help you build a focused, enjoyable collection.

🏆

Exhibits & Competitions

Show your collection and learn from others. Members regularly exhibit at regional and national-level competitions.

📚

Reference Library

Access to our growing collection of catalogs, journals, and reference materials covering U.S. and worldwide philately.

💌

Member Auctions

Bid on quality material at our regular member auctions — a great way to expand your collection and support the club.

🎟️

EVANSPEX Show

Help organize Indiana's longest-running stamp and postcard show, with dealers, exhibits, and a USPS cancellation booth.
