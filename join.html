Membership

Become a member.

For less than the cost of a single classic stamp, you'll join a community that's been together for nearly a century. Membership supports the club, EVANSPEX, and our beginner outreach programs.

Not ready to join? Just come to a meeting first. Visitors are always welcome and there's no pressure to sign up.
